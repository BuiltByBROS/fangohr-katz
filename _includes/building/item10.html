52 Thomas Street

New York, NY

  • Building Type: Residential Condo, 120 units
  • Services Provided: Architectural Consulting Services

7-story Landmark District loft building composed of four buildings constructed in 1915 as shoe factories and combined as a Condo in 2006. The project addresses replacement of a deficient building-wide mechanical system and LPC objections regarding visibility of equipment. Code and zoning investigation, construction document production, LPC submission and presentations.
